20th Century Girl

A Story of 1999
Why I love it

I love this movie because of its 20th-century aesthetic and the beautiful way it portrays youth and first love. The story is lighthearted at first, but it carries a depth that stays with you long after the credits roll.

The Heartbreaking Truth

The most painful part for me was the ending. After Poong Woon-ho left, the long wait of Na Bo-ra felt so heavy.

It was only when she was older that she finally discovered the truth: he had passed away a long time ago.

That revelation was devastating. It makes you realize how some goodbyes are permanent without us even knowing it. The video tape at the end, showing his perspective of that summer, made the heartbreak even more real.
